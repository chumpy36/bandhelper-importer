BandHelper Song Importer

A small command-line tool that bulk-creates songs for BandHelper (which has no API) by generating ChordPro and tab-delimited import files. It auto-fills song duration, lyrics, tempo (BPM) and key, and converts Ultimate Guitar chord charts into inline ChordPro.

Source code: github.com/chumpy36/bandhelper-importer

Tempo (BPM) and song key data provided by GetSongBPM
https://getsongbpm.com
